fn ascii_chars(&self) -> AsciiCharsIter

Iterate over Unicode characters converted to ASCII sequences.

Items of this iterator may be None for some characters. Use .map(|ch| ch.unwrap_or("?")) to replace invalid characters.

fn to_ascii_lossy(&self) -> String

Convert any Unicode string to ASCII-only string.

Characters are converted to closest ASCII equivalent. Characters that can't be converted are replaced with "[?]".
